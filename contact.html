Frequently Asked Questions

What is your design process?

My design process typically involves discovery, research, conceptualization, design development, revisions, and final delivery. I believe in collaborative work and keep clients involved throughout the process.

How long does a project take?

Project timelines vary depending on scope and complexity. A logo design might take 2-3 weeks, while a complete brand identity could take 4-6 weeks. I'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

What are your payment terms?

I typically require a 50% deposit to begin work, with the remaining balance due upon project completion. For larger projects, I offer milestone-based payment schedules.

Do you offer revisions?

Yes, all projects include 2-3 rounds of revisions. Additional revisions can be arranged at an hourly rate if needed.
