About Me

Hello, I'm Sampda Gangurde

I'm a passionate graphic designer and illustrator based in New York City with over 5 years of experience in the creative industry.

My journey in design began when I was just a child, drawing and creating art whenever I could. This passion led me to pursue a Bachelor's degree in Graphic Design from the School of Visual Arts, where I honed my skills and developed my unique style.

I believe that great design has the power to tell stories, evoke emotions, and create meaningful connections. My approach combines creativity with strategic thinking to deliver designs that not only look beautiful but also effectively communicate your message.

When I'm not designing, you can find me exploring art galleries, practicing photography, or hiking in nature to find inspiration for my next project.
